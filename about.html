About

I am a Doctoral Researcher at the Societal Computing Group, Saarland University, Germany, which is part of the [Interdisciplinary Institute for Societal Computing](https://www.i2sc.net). My work lies in the intersection of society and the digital world, focusing mainly on Politics, Hate Speech and Misinformation. My research combines computational methods with social sciences to understand the dynamics of online communities and the society at large. Education and Other roles: * PhD in Computer Science, [Saarland University](https://www.uni-saarland.de/start.html), Germany (2023 - Present) * Research Fellow, [Department of Peace Operations, United Nations](https://peacekeeping.un.org/en/department-of-peace-operations) (Nov 2023 - May 2024) * Robotics Researcher, [University of Hyderabad](https://uohyd.ac.in), India (2022) * Integrated M.Tech in Computer Science, [University of Hyderabad](https://uohyd.ac.in), India (2017-2022) Service to the Profession: * Reviewer, [Workshop on Online Abuse and Harms, ACL](https://www.workshopononlineabuse.com). * Reviewer, [COLING, ACL](https://aclanthology.org/venues/coling/) Teaching Assistant: * Elements of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, Saarland University (2023 - Present) * Project Seminar: Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, Saarland University (2023 - Present)
