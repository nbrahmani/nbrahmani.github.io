About

I am a Doctoral Researcher at the Societal Computing Group, Saarland University, Saarbrücken, Germany, also part of the [Interdisciplinary Institute for Societal Computing](https://www.i2sc.net). My work lies in the intersection of society and the digital world, focusing mainly on Politics, Hate Speech and Misinformation. My research combines computational methods with social sciences to understand the dynamics of online communities and the society at large.
